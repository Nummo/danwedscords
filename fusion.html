The Fusion

West African evening ceremony & attire

#DanwedsCords


Attire

In keeping with Cordelia’s West African heritage aspects of the evening party will see the joining of the 2 families with a traditional ashobi ceremony.

2 different Ankara fabric prints have been chosen & released by Cordelia and Dan for their respective family and friends.

The union of two families is something special and they cannot wait to spend the day and evening celebrating with you. If you would like to have some of the print sent out to you to make an ashobi outfit of your own for the evening ceremony then please do just let Dan and Cordelia know at danwedscords@gmail.com. Please note this is not essential but the more that get involved….the merrier:)


African Ashobi ceremony

For those of you that have chosen to make an outfit for the evening celebrations, please be rest assured you will not have to carry around the outfit all day ;) There will be a collection point at the church and your outfits, which will be transported to the reception venue where it can be collected. There will also be a facility for everyone to change after the wedding breakfast.
