Transportation

After party transportation

Tube: The nearest station is Canning Town which is on the jubilee line and operates as part of the TFL night tube. It is approx a 15 min walk from the venue (1 mile) and can also be accessed via taxi which is 5 mins, with a journey time of 5 mins from the venue.

Shuttles: Evening carriages will be provided between 00:30 - 02:00. These will run to the Ibis, Crowne Plaza and The Good hotel at excel.

Parking:

Please note there is no parking available at the church and very limited parking available at the venue, which have been reserved. Car parking has been made available at Royal Victoria, ExCeL London - which is in the same vicinity as the:

  • Good Hotel London
  • Crowne Plaza London Docklands
  • Hotel ibis London Excel Docklands


A shuttle service will be operating a drop off to the car park and the hotels, between 00:30 - 02:00. If you are wanting to drive to the wedding then we kindly ask that you:
For the all day guests (church + party) we advise that you park your car at the car park prior to making your way to the church service, as transportation will be provided from the church to the wedding reception venue and then from the wedding reception venue to the car park at the end of the night.
If you have any questions regarding car parking and reserved spaces please contact danwedscords@gmail.com

Hotel

The Good Hotel London is the chosen hotel for the wedding.

Please use the following code to enable a discount of 10% of the evening rate: DanwedsCords

Other hotels available in the same area as the good hotel are as follows:

  • Crowne Plaza London Docklands
  • Hotel ibis London Excel Docklands

All hotels are approx. 7 min drive from the venue for the reception.
